About

I am a computer science student specializing in cybersecurity and ethical hacking. At the age of 21, I live in Abidjan. I am currently studying computer systems and network administration at the UVCI, and I am simultaneously training in offensive security and web application development. My goal is to become a Red Teamer.
On this portfolio, you will discover my projects and my skills in offensive security (Pentesting and Red Teaming, CTF) and general computer science. I hope you enjoy your visit and feel free to contact me if you have any questions or proposals for training or collaboration.
